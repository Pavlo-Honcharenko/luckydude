How It Works

Luckydudes bring good luck and prosperity to those who hold them, and are automatically entered in raffles each week for the chance to win life-changing prizes. Here's how it works:
Mint a Luckydude. Your new Dude comes preloaded with 20 raffle tokens.
Your Dude will be automatically entered into the raffles every Tuesday and Friday. Each Dude represents $5 USD into the raffle prize pool — so for example, if there are 10,000 active Dudes today, the prize pool will be $50,000.
When the raffle occurs, one Luckydude is randomly selected to be the grand prize winner. You'll get a message showing you which Dude won. Also, if your Dude has the same Color, Location or Name as the winner, you receive a secondary prize.
  • 70% of the prize pool goes to the winning Dude
  • 30% of the prize pool is distributed among the secondary prize winners

Refer to Earn

Invite your friends to Luckydude and you'll be generously rewarded through our refer-to-earn protocol:
Direct Referrals:
For every friend that mints a Luckydude using your invite code, you'll get a $25 reward for making a Direct Referral; plus, if that Dude you referred wins the grand prize anytime in the future, you'll get 4% of the prize pool.
Secondary Referrals:
When the friends who you referred then go invite their own friends, you get credit for Secondary Referrals — you'll get a $5 reward for each Secondary Referral, plus you'll get 1% of the prize pool if one of your secondary referrals wins the raffle.

FAQ

How do I get a Luckydude?
Buying a Luckydude is simple (whether you've bought NFTs before or not) — just follow these steps:
  • If you already have a crypto wallet, you'll need to have enough MATIC to purchase the Luckydude ($220 USD). You can purchase MATIC on Coinbase, or you can convert other cryptocurrencies you own into MATIC. Once you have the MATIC, make sure it's in the wallet that you're using to connect to the Luckydude site. Click «Mint» and follow the on-screen instructions.
  • If you do not have a crypto wallet, the first step is to create one. The easiest method is to create a Coinbase account — from there, you can both buy MATIC and use the Coinbase wallet (with the new MATIC in it) to connect to the Luckydude site. Click here for a detailed step-by-step explanation.
Can I mint a Luckydude using other tokens?
No – as of now, Luckydude is only transacting on the Polygon blockchain and you need to have MATIC coin in order to mint. We've chosen Polygon to limit the gas fees you'll pay when minting Luckydudes and receiving your prizes.
How much does it cost to mint a Luckydude?
To mint a Luckydude costs [X]ETH ($220 USD). The Luckydude NFT itself costs $120 USD, and it comes preloaded with 20 raffles (20 raffles X $5 per raffle = $100 USD).
How many raffles do I get?
When you mint a Luckydude it will be active for 20 consecutive raffles (10 weeks). After that time, you can reload the Dude to keep it active and participating in the raffle drawings.
How big is the raffle prize?
The prize pool is determined by the number of Dudes entered that day. Each Dude represents $5 to the prize pool. So for example, if 50,000 dudes are participating today, the prize pool will be $250,000 (50,000 Dudes X $5).
What do I get if I win a secondary prize?
If your Dude is the same color as the winning Dude, you win 1 extra raffle token ($5 value)
If your Dude is from the same location as the winning Dude, you win ~$150 (exact amount calculated based on the number of actual winners – all Location winners split 12% of the prize pool)
If your Dude has the same name as the winning Dude, you win ~$500 (exact amount calculated based on the number of actual winners – all Name winners split 5% of the prize pool)
What is the distribution of Dude attributes?
Each Dude is a specific color (20 unique colors), is from a specific location (200 unique locations), and has a specific name (2000 unique names).
How will I know if I win?
If you win a prize, you will be notified via email and when you claim the prize it will be sent directly to your wallet. All prizes can be withdrawn instantly, and are distributed in MATIC.
Do I have to manually enter each raffle?
No – once you buy your Dude, you are automatically entered into the raffle drawings for as long as your Dude remains active.
Can I own more than one Dude?
Yes, you can buy as many Dudes as you like. The more Dudes you own, the better your chances of winning. Each Dude has a completely independent chance of being selected.
What do I get for inviting friends?
If a friend mints a Dude through your referral, you get credit for a Direct Referral and receive a $25 referral bonus. In addition, if a Dude you invited ever wins the grand prize at any time in future, you'll get 4% of the prize pool as a referral prize! There is no limit to the number of friends you can invite. Make sure to give your friends your Dude's ID number so they can enter it (to give you credit) when they mint their Dudes. In order to be eligible for referral prizes you must own a Luckydude.
What are Secondary Referrals?
If a Dude that you invited then goes and invites additional Dudes, you get credit for those Secondary Referrals. You'll receive a $5 reward for each Secondary Referral – plus, if any of those Secondary Referrals Dudes win the raffle in the future, you'll get 1% of the prize pool!
When do the raffles happen?
Raffles occur every Tuesday and Friday at 2pm EST.
How does the drawing work?
Each participating Dude has an equal chance of being selected as the winner. The selection algorithm is powered by 3rd-party Chainlink VRF tool – the selection and prize distribution is on-chain and fully transparent. The game mechanics including the selection mechanism has been audited and approved by [AUDITOR], deeming the game provably fair and transparent.
How many Luckydudes are available?
There are 10,000 special Luckydudes being released in the first phase. This first set of 10,000 are “OG Dudes”, and come with special benefits [describe].
Beyond this initial release, we hope to grow the community to millions of Luckydudes – the more Dudes participating in each raffle, the larger the prize pool. For example, let's say there were one million Luckydudes participating in today's raffle – the prize pool would be $5M!
Can I sell or transfer my Dude?
You can sell or transfer your Dude just like any other NFT. Though every Dude has an equal chance of winning the raffle, some people like to have Dudes with specific attributes.
